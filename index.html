ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Via Celis, a 3rd year Information Technology student who turns caffeine into code, design ideas into digital reality, and messy data into meaningful insights. I'm naturally creative, curious, focused, and analytical (basically I overthink everything, but in a productive way). I'm passionate about the visual side of technology and making sense of data - because let's be honest, nobody wants to use an ugly website or stare at confusing spreadsheets. I love transforming wild ideas into engaging interfaces, analyzing data patterns, and bringing designs to life through code, whether I'm crafting user-centered designs in Figma, convincing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to play nice together, or finding stories hidden in datasets.
